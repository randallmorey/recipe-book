Rustic Bread

A rustic no-knead bread goes well with soups, stews, or just with some butter. Ingredients 500 grams all purpose flour 380 grams warm water 10 grams salt 1 gram dry active yeast Steps Mix all ingredients in a large bowl. This will be a wet dough.
